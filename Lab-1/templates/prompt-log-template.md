## Instructions

Copy this template for each experiment session. Fill in one row of the table per API call, and use the Notes column to record observations. Attach your completed prompt log to homework submissions.

The goal is not bureaucratic record-keeping — it is building your intuition about what works and why. Students who log carefully in the early weeks make much better model selection decisions in the later weeks.
